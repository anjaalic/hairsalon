About Us

Located in the heart of Inglewood, this warm and friendly vintage retreat full of quirky charm will take you on a journey back in time…to old-fashioned personalised service of the finest standard, where we create bespoke looks that will suit your individual style and personality. Artsy Hairess is the perfect place to relax in the hands of your professional stylist or therapist and let yourself drift away from the daily grind of work. Skilled Owner/Director Natalie Vidal has had over 20 years of experience in the hairdressing industry, including several years in Melbourne, where she committed herself to the highest standard of training in every part of her profession, also completing two make-up courses enabling her to freelance as a hair and make-up artist for numerous weddings upon her move back to Perth.
